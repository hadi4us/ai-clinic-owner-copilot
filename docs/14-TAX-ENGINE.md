# 14. Tax Engine

## Status

Future roadmap. Not Phase 1.

## Purpose

Tax Engine will help estimate tax-related business summaries from approved finance metrics.

## Guardrails

- Not a replacement for accountant/tax consultant.
- No tax filing automation in MVP.
- Must keep tenant-scoped calculations.
- Must distinguish estimate from official tax report.

## Future Inputs

- Revenue category.
- Expense category.
- Entity/tax profile.
- Period.
- Applicable tax rules.
